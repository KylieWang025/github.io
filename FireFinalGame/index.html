You are a creature with the ability to throw fire. Unfortunately you have lost your fire. Travel the lands using the arrow keys to find your fire again.

Only after you and your fire reunite do you gain the ability to shoot fire, and jump over puddles. Use WASD to control the direction you throw your fire. The world you live in has only certain objects you can burn through, mainly trees.

Travel to the portal to teleport to other parts of the land.
Have fun!
